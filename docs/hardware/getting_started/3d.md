## Before Printing

> We assume you have basic experience with consumer-level 3D printers (K1 Max, Ender 3, etc.). This means you know how to properly 3D print STL files with PLA filaments and are familiar with reorienting parts, adding supports, adjusting infill, and modifying print speed to achieve your desired balance of material strength, efficiency, and model detail quality.
>

### Cost Considerations

* **Own printer** (Recommended): Everything can be printed on consumer 3D printers like the Creality K1 Max (~$899.00 USD).
* **Filament cost**: Approximately $38–55 USD for the required PLA filament.
